Meet the Designer

Camille Conaway-Heffner is a Lititz PA based designer who launched her leather jewelry and accessories line Camilley Girl in 2017.
Camille works with calf, kid, and lamb skin to make easy-to-wear pieces: many which are one of a kind.
Camille attended the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City and graduated with a bachelors degree in accessories design and fabrication. This accomplishment was the fulfillment of her childhood dream of learning how to work with leather to make shoes, handbags and varies accessories from concept to finished product.

Where to Shop

Lititz, PA. Online.

You can find Camilley Girl at various craft shows and shops in eastern Pennsylvania and on Etsy.com. One staple location is the Purple Robin Reserve on Main street of downtown Lititz, PA. Camille is active in the community and can often be seen dropping off new product and greeting customers.
