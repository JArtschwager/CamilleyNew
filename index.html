Meet the Designer

NYC Trained, Lititz PA Trained

Camille Conaway-Heffner is a Lititz PA based designer who launched her leather jewelry and accessories line Camilley Girl in 2017. Camille works with calf, kid, and lamb skin to make easy-to-wear pieces: many which are one of a kind.
Camille attended the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City and graduated with a bachelors degree in accessories design and fabrication. This accomplishment was the fulfillment of her childhood dream of learning how to work with leather to make shoes and handbags from concept to finished product. You can find Camilley Girl at various shops in eastern Pennsylvania and Etsy.com.
